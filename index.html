What is NLP & and why should we Care?

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Natural Language Processing (NLP) refers to a subset of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that is interested in having computers be able to understand and interact with text and speech. Most people use some form of NLP in their everyday lives without thinking about it with tools such as search results, Email filters, autocorrect, and smart assistants such as Amazon’s Alexa and Apple’s Siri.

Gender Binary & Implications

NLP models often use gendered information in order to make decisions whether that be within translations or recognition, however they typically only use a male-female split to do so; effectivily erasing anyone who is not a cis-gendered man or woman. Some of the harms associated with theses decisions include “resume scanning systems throwing out resumes from non-binary persons for not having a recognizable name, undercounting instances of discrimination against non-binary persons, and incorrectly processing gender in medical documents, leading to incorrect care” (Dev, 6).
